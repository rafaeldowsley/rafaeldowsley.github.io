About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Center for Research in Applied Cryptography and Cyber Security at the Bar-Ilan University. My research interests lie in cryptography and its many intersections with other fields such as information theory, machine learning and privacy.

Previously I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Cryptography and Security Research Group at the Aarhus University. I got my Doctoral degree in the Institute of Theoretical Informatics at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology where I worked in the Cryptography and Security Working Group under Professor Jörn Müller-Quade. My thesis is available here. Before that, I obtained a Bachelor's degree in Communication Networks Engineering and a Master's degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Brasília (UnB), Brazil. My advisor was Professor Anderson C. A. Nascimento. I also obtained a Master's degree in Computer Science at the University of California at San Diego working with Professor Mihir Bellare.
