About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Center for Research in Applied Cryptography and Cyber Security at the Bar-Ilan University. My research interests lie in cryptography and its many intersections with machine learning, information theory and privacy. Previously I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Cryptography and Security Research Group at the Aarhus University and got my doctoral degree from the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, where I worked in the Cryptography and Security Working Group under Professor Jörn Müller-Quade. My thesis is available here.
